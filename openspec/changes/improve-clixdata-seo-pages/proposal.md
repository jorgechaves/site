## Why

The current ClixData site presents strong service content on a single page, but it limits organic search reach because each service cannot target its own high-intent keywords and shareable metadata. This change improves discoverability for searches around consultoria em Business Intelligence, dashboards Power BI, SQL, Python, ETL/ELT, administracao de banco de dados, engenharia de dados, and infraestrutura de dados.

## What Changes

- Add dedicated SEO-friendly service URLs for Business Intelligence, Banco de Dados, ETL/ELT, and Infraestrutura de Dados.
- Update page titles, meta descriptions, headings, and natural service copy to target commercial pt-BR search intent.
- Add Open Graph and social sharing metadata, including a branded social preview image.
- Improve structured data with Organization, ProfessionalService/Service, ContactPoint, logo, area served, and service catalog details.
- Add crawl support files: `robots.txt` and `sitemap.xml`.
- Optimize logo/social image usage for performance and accessibility.
- Add trust-building content such as problems solved, service-specific FAQs, sectors, technologies, and truthful examples of expected dashboard/data outcomes without fake testimonials or fabricated metrics.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- `seo-service-pages`: Dedicated SEO landing pages, metadata, crawl files, structured data, image optimization, and trust content for ClixData's service offerings.

### Modified Capabilities
None.

## Impact

- Affected files: `index.html`, `assets/css/styles.css`, `assets/js/main.js`, new static service pages or route-equivalent HTML files, `robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`, and optimized image assets.
- SEO impact: more targeted crawlable URLs, better snippets, richer social previews, and improved structured data for search engines.
- UX impact: visitors can land directly on the service they searched for, then navigate back to contact and related services.
- Deployment impact: static hosting must serve clean URLs such as `/business-intelligence`, `/banco-de-dados`, `/etl-elt`, and `/infraestrutura-de-dados`.
